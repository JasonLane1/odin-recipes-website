Halushki

Polish dish combining bacon, fried cabbage and egg noodles.

Nutrition Facts

Calories Fat Carbs Protein
698 38g 69g 22g

Ingredients

  • 1 pound bacon
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 1(16 ounce) package egg noodles
  • 1 head cabbage, sliced
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Directions

Step 1

Snip bacon into small pieces with a scissors and cook in a large skillet
medium heat until crisp, stirring often, about 10 minutes. Cook and stir
with bacon until translucent, about 5 more minutes; set bacon and onion
aside, leaving drippings in the skillet.

Step 2

Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook egg noodles in
the boiling water, stirring occasionally until cooked through but firm to
the bite, about 5 minutes. Drain.

Step 3

Transfer bacon and onion mixture with drippings into the pot used to
cook the noodles and cook and stir cabbage until coated with drippings.
Cover pot and cook until cabbage is tender, 10 to 12 minutes, stirring
occasionally. Gently stir in noodles and season to taste with salt and
black pepper.
